Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an insurance license to attend? ▼
No! The Business Overview is open to everyone. If you decide to move forward, we'll help you get licensed. Most people are fully licensed in about 3 weeks.
Is there any cost to attend? ▼
The Business Overview is completely free. No obligation — come learn, ask questions, and decide if it's right for you.
How much does it cost to get started? ▼
Kairos charges absolutely nothing to get started. The only costs are state licensing fees ($250–$500 depending on your state). You should also set aside $1,000–$1,500 for leads and marketing once licensed.
How long does it take to get licensed? ▼
About 3 weeks. Pre-licensing course, state exam, and carrier appointments. We walk you through every step.
Is this really 100% remote? ▼
Yes. Agents in all 50 states work from home — and many work remotely from around the world. All trainings, meetings, and mentorship happen virtually.
What if I have zero sales experience? ▼
Many top agents came in with no sales background. Our mentorship program takes you from zero to producing agent fast.
How long is the Business Overview? ▼
About 45–60 minutes, followed by live Q&A.
What kind of income can I expect? ▼
Part-time agents often earn $3K–$5K/month. Full-time agents regularly exceed $10K+/month within their first year.
